## Key context, in one paragraph

At the April 7 meeting we agreed on the process skeleton: SPARC question 5 as input, an Oct 18 congregation-wide input service, a small drafting group, at least one feedback round, vote by year end. **Tonight is about three things: what we want to *get out of* the Oct 18 service, who the drafters are, and whether we add parallel youth programming.**

A useful reframe to keep in mind: CUUC already has a **mission, a covenant, Shared Values, and an 8th Principle** that say a great deal about who we are and how we relate. The vision we're producing should sit *alongside* those four documents and answer the remaining question: *who do we become if we live all of the above for 5–10 years?*

## What we're taking as given tonight

- **Oct 18 will be the main congregational input event.** Not in question.
- The new vision sits alongside the existing four CUUC documents (above).
- The drafting group will be small (~2–3); the question tonight is *who* and *how chosen*, not whether to have one.

## Decisions Alex's slot wants to land on tonight

1. What kind of inputs do we most want the Oct 18 service to produce? (aspirations, concrete imagery, reactions to themes, concerns, past-moment stories — some combination.)
2. What feel should the service have on the worship ↔ workshop spectrum?
3. What's our supplementary-input channel for people who can't attend Oct 18?
4. How big is the drafting committee? *(Default: 2–3.)*
5. What criteria do drafters need to meet?
6. How are drafters selected (COM appoints / minister recommends / open call / hybrid)?
7. Parallel youth visioning track on Oct 18, or integrated kids-in-sanctuary?

## Explicitly *not* deciding tonight

- Order of service or specific format design (Alex synthesizes after tonight; June reviews a concrete plan).
- Final wording of input questions (deferred to June, alongside broader SPARC data).
- Specific names of drafters.
- The text of the new vision statement.
- Final vote date.
