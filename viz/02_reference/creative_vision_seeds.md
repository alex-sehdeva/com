# Creative Vision Seeds

A set of deliberately unusual, compressed, or experimental vision statements — generated as a creative exercise (via ChatGPT) — kept here to **push the committee toward a richer possibility space** than the standard UU template.

These are **not candidates** for adoption. They are conversation-loosening prompts — useful when the COM or the drafting team needs to break out of the "welcoming + spiritual growth + justice" formula and notice what else a vision statement could sound like.

**How to use them:** read alongside [`example_uu_vision_statements.md`](example_uu_vision_statements.md). Notice which of these resonate, which feel wrong, which feel surprisingly close to something true. The contrast is the point.
